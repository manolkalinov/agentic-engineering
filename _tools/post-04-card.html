AGENTIC ENGINEERING — POST 4
Escalation Levels: Calibrate Your AI's Autonomy
L0 Autonomous — just do it
L1 Do it, tell me — proceed, then explain
L2 Propose first — plan it, wait for approval
L3 Full stop — flag the risk, decide together
Hobby project? You probably don't need L3.
Production SaaS? Almost nothing without a human in the loop.

One page per repo. Calibrate to your stakes.
